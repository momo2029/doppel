# Doppel - Your AI Social Doppelgänger

**Doppel** is a Chrome extension that automates social media interactions while maintaining your unique personality. Let AI handle the social grind, but keep it authentically *you*.

## 🌟 Features

- **Multi-Platform Support**: Works on Weibo, Xiaohongshu, Zhihu, Douban, Reddit, Twitter/X, and more
- **Personality DNA System**: AI learns your writing style, interests, and tone from your history
- **Smart Patrol**: Automatically browses feeds and interacts based on your preferences
- **Authentic Replies**: Generates comments that sound like you, not a bot
- **Plugin Marketplace**: Extend functionality with community plugins

## 📥 Download

Download the latest version from [Releases](https://github.com/momo2029/doppel/releases):
- **Latest**: [doppel-latest.zip](https://github.com/momo2029/doppel/releases/download/latest/doppel-latest.zip)

### Installation

1. Download `doppel-latest.zip`
2. Unzip the file
3. Open Chrome → Extensions → Enable "Developer mode"
4. Click "Load unpacked" → Select the unzipped folder
5. Done! 🎉

## 🚀 How It Works

1. **Import Your History**: Upload your past posts/comments
2. **AI Analyzes**: Doppel learns your personality, interests, and writing patterns
3. **Set Preferences**: Choose platforms, interaction frequency, and topics
4. **Auto Patrol**: Doppel browses and interacts on your behalf
5. **Stay Authentic**: All replies match your unique voice
